title: Wrap-up
---

Uniqueness of composites is the workhorse of this section. It says the chosen composite is the *only* witnessed composite, so any triangle you can build pins its hypotenuse to it. The two unit laws are immediate corollaries: the degenerate unit triangles witness `f` as a composite with the identity, and uniqueness identifies that composite with `f`.

These laws are part of what makes a Segal type behave like a category. They also feed the directed development ahead: the left unit law closes the easy round-trip of the Yoneda equivalence, and the right unit law simplifies the naturality square for representable transformations.
