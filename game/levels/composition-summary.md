title: Wrap-up
---

In a Segal type, composition exists and comes with a witnessing triangle, the two halves of one center of contraction. That is what makes a Segal type a **pre-$(\infty,1)$-category**. A **Rezk type** (a Segal type that is also locally univalent) is then a genuine $(\infty,1)$-category.
