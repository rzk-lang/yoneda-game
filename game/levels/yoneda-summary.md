---

You proved the contravariant Yoneda lemma: for objects `a` and `b` in a pre-∞-category, evaluation at the identity is an equivalence between fiberwise transformations of representables and the arrows `a → b`. A whole transformation is the same data as a single arrow.

The proof reused the entire game. The inverse map is composition, from the Segal structure of the early directed chapters. The easy round-trip is the left unit law. The hard round-trip is naturality, proved automatic in the previous section, together with the right unit law and the path algebra of the opening chapters, glued by function extensionality. The capstone simply packages the two round-trips as a retraction and a section.

This is the destination of the geodesic. What remains is optional: the naturality of the equivalence itself, and breadth such as associativity, neither of which the headline lemma needs.
