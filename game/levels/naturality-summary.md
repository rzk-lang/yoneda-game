---

You proved that every fiberwise transformation between representable functors is automatically natural. No naturality hypothesis was needed: the Segal structure supplies it. The argument was a single square, built from a composition witness and a unit triangle, transported through `ϕ`, then read back as two composition relations and glued by uniqueness of composites, the right unit law, and path concatenation.

This is the technical heart of the contravariant Yoneda lemma. With naturality automatic, the Yoneda maps can be defined without carrying a separate naturality proof, and the equivalence between a representable transformation and its value at the identity follows next.
