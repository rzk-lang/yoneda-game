The identity morphism does nothing, so composing with it should change nothing. Proving that needs one new tool: in a Segal type the composite is not merely *a* filler but *the* filler. Any triangle you can build over a composable pair identifies its hypotenuse with the chosen composite. This is **uniqueness of composites**. Feed it the two degenerate unit triangles and the **unit laws** fall out.

*By the end you will be able to:* show that any witnessed composite equals the chosen one, and read off the right and left unit laws from it.
