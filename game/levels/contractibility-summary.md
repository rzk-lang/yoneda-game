title: Wrap-up
---

You can read an `is-contr` proof: its first projection is the center, its second is the contracting homotopy from the center to every point. Combining contraction paths shows that any two points are equal. The Segal condition, soon, phrases composition as the center of a contractible type, and the uniqueness of composites is exactly this contraction.
