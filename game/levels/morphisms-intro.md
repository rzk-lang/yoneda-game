In directed type theory a **morphism** $x \to y$ is a path along the directed interval $\Delta^1$, and a **triangle** (`hom2`) is a map out of the $2$-simplex $\Delta^2$. This first section builds them by hand.

*By the end you will be able to:* construct the identity morphism, fill the constant triangle, and reparametrize an edge to fill a degenerate (unit) triangle. The mirror **left-unit** triangle is marked ★ (optional enrichment you may skip).
